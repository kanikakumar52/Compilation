Heart rate versus horror movie. For this project, I decided to watch movies and record my heart rate at set intervals to naturally react to a horror movie. I picked three very different movies and recorded two viewers' reactions. I want to introduce a new way to review movies, where the viewer provides their own review in addition to evidence of how they subconsciously reacted to the movie.
